About Veliqent

Technology should make
work feel more intentional.

Veliqent Labs is an independent technology studio focused on custom software platforms, business applications, automation, integrations, data, and practical digital systems.

We build with Python, JavaScript and TypeScript, modern web technologies, PostgreSQL, APIs, and cloud services. We also use Microsoft Power Platform when it is the right fit—choosing tools according to the problem rather than forcing every project into one platform.

We are building our capabilities in public through disciplined learning, reusable solutions, and transparent project work.
